Chocolate Chip Gooey Delicious Cookies!

Straight From Martha Stewart Home Living :)

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vailla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups (about 12 ounces) semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Baking Instructions!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda, set aside
  3. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, combine the butter with both; beat on medium speed until light and fluffy
  4. Reduce speed to low; add the salt, vanilla, and eggs
  5. Beat until well mixed, about 1 minute
  6. Add flour mixture; mix until just combined
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips
  8. Drop heaping tablespoon-size balls of dough about 2 inches apart on baking sheets lined with parchment paper
  9. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes
  10. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes
  11. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely
  12. Store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature up to 1 week
