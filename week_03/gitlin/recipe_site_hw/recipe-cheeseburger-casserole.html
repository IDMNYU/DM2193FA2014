    Cheeseburger Casserole Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1/2 cup ketchup
    • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
    • 1/4 cup bbq sauce (optional)
    • 1 cup cheddar cheese
    • Tater tots or french fries

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 425° F
    2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet until no pink remains.
    3. Drain the ground beef.
    4. Mix ketchup, mustard and bbq sauce into meat until meat is evenly coated.
    5. Pour meat into a caserole dish, creating an even layer on the bottom.
    6. Place an even layer of tots or fries over the meat.
    7. Evenly cover meat and tots/fries with cheese.
    8. Cook in the oven for 15~20 minutes or until tots/fries are fully cooked.
