Making Chocolate Chip Cookies With Just a Microwave!

With a Bunch of Other Stuff

Ingredients!

  • 1 Tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon (pinch) salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1-2 Tablespoons chocolate chips

Super Complicated Instructions

  1. Microwave butter in a mug until melted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  2. With a spoon, mix in sugars, vanilla extract and salt until well-combined.
  3. Mix in egg yolk until no traces of the yolk are seen. Mix in flour.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Microwave on high for 40 seconds, or a maximum of 50 seconds.
  6. Serve warm (maybe even with a dollop of ice cream).

Enjoy!
