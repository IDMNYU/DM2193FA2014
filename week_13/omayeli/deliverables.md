### Tips

##### <a name="filenames"></a>Filenames
Guidelines for good file names for web development
* all lower case
* no spaces
* no punctation `!*(){}[]#@$%^&`
  * hyphens and underscores are ok
* only alphanumerics `[a-z][1-9]`
