      Final (Update 1)

      We've made our markers and tested them for detection. They work now. We've also sorted out the features of our Augumented Reality Lookbook (the name is in the works). For now, we have five items we are planning to display -- skirt, bag, shoe, shirt, dress and trousers (pants? I don't know what you Americans call it.) We are sticking with this for now because while there is a wide array of amazing 3d models available, a lot of the good ones cost money. So...yeah. Other features we're hoping to get to are:

      • Buttons to go back and forth btw colors
      • "Do you want to try this on?" button
      • QR code to link to website
      • Floating text to describe the item
      • A well-designed lookbook instead of a pile of papers
      • Price button
      • Control rotation
